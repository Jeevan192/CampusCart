FAQ

Frequently asked points from first-time users.

Can I search first and login later?

Yes. Query intent is stored and automatically applied after sign in and marketplace entry.

How do I know who is selling an item?

Seller details appear in product detail view, enabling immediate verification and contact.

What if I am not ready to buy now?

Wishlist helps track options over time before making a final purchase decision.
